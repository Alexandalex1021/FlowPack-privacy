FlowPack 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

欢迎使用 FlowPack！我们非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们如何处理与保护您的信息。

Welcome to FlowPack! We value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle and protect your information.

1. 数据收集 / Data Collection

FlowPack 不会收集、存储或上传任何可识别个人身份的信息。您的学习数据、设置与进度仅保存在您的本地设备或 iCloud 账户中。

FlowPack does not collect, store, or upload any personally identifiable information. Your learning data, settings, and progress are stored only on your local device or in your personal iCloud account.

2. 数据使用 / Data Usage

应用中的数据仅用于提供学习功能与统计展示，不会被开发者或任何第三方访问或共享。

All data within the app is used solely to provide learning features and display statistics. No data is accessed or shared by the developer or any third party.

3. 第三方服务 / Third-Party Services

FlowPack 使用 Apple 的 iCloud 与 StoreKit 服务，用于同步学习进度及管理内购订阅。所有数据传输均受 Apple 的隐私保护机制保障。

FlowPack uses Apple's iCloud and StoreKit services to sync learning progress and manage in-app purchases. All data transmission is protected under Apple’s privacy and security framework.
